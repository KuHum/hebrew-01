Aleph with Beth

Version 1.0 • Study Tracker

Aleph with Beth is a free, offline‑first study tracker based on the Aleph with Beth curriculum. It helps you work through the video lessons day‑by‑day, check off tasks, record quiz scores, and never lose your place – even without internet.

Features

• Dynamic schedule from Aleph with Beth / Alpha with Angela
• Offline‑first – works entirely without internet after first visit
• Fillable blanks for quiz scores
• Progress tracking with percentage and visual bars
• Export / import progress to back up or move devices
• Installable as a standalone app
