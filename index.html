Learn Biblical Hebrew

Version 1.0 • Study Tracker

Learn Biblical Hebrew is a free, offline‑first study tracker based on the Aleph with Beth curriculum. It helps you work through the video lessons day‑by‑day, check off tasks, record quiz scores, and never lose your place – even without internet.

Features

• 52‑week structured schedule (310 study days)
• Offline‑first – works entirely without internet after first visit
• Fillable blanks for quiz scores
• Progress tracking with percentage and visual bars
• Export / import progress to back up or move devices
• Installable as a standalone app

What Makes This Curriculum Special

Aleph with Beth uses the Comprehensible Input method – the same natural way you learned your first language. No tedious grammar drills, no memorising rules. Just watch, listen, and gradually understand. You start with basic words and phrases without any text, just like a child learning to speak. The alphabet is introduced slowly after you’ve built a foundation of understanding. This stress‑free approach lets your brain absorb the language at your own pace, from the comfort of home. It’s free forever, designed to serve the global Church and level the playing field for everyone.
